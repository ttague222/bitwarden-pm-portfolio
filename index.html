Flagship recommendation

Repair first, then extend.

The program sequence is intentionally conservative. Guidance built on an untrusted surface compounds irritation; extension should be earned by repair, transparency, and measurement.

  1. 01

    Repair

    Dismiss, snooze, and explain every warning without hiding real risk.

  2. 02

    Transparency

    Publish the at-risk methodology as a community RFC with a disposition table.

  3. 03

    Measurement

    Build a Premium value readout before the January 2027 renewal moment.

  4. 04

    Earned Extension

    Pilot guided resolution, setup recovery, and factual value summaries behind flags.

Who this serves

One guidance surface, four correct behaviors.

These personas are not invented demographics. Each is a behavioral segment that surfaced repeatedly in the public research, named by what the person does and anchored to the evidence that proves they exist. The same feature must behave differently for each of them.

P1

The Switcher

Just imported 400 items from Chrome or 1Password. Autofill still off. One bad week from sliding back to the browser default.

Needs: setup recovery, sequenced warnings, and a duplicate-free vault. The checklist is their surface.

Evidence: 8 new importers shipped; save-prompt defect #1620; 466 votes for duplicate tooling.

P2

The Daily Retriever

The silent majority. Opens the app to get one credential and leave. Has never seen Reports. Most damaged by regressions and un-dismissible prompts.

Needs: reliability and restraint. Guidance appears rarely, explains itself, and stays dismissed.

Evidence: top open issues are all daily-path friction; popup delay #12286; unlock re-prompt complaints.

P3

The Community Auditor

Self-hosts, reads the source, votes on the forum, and screenshots anything that smells like a dark pattern. A minority of users; the majority of the public voice.

Needs: agency, reasons, inspectable logic. The published methodology and RFC are their feature.

Evidence: 973-post redesign megathread; 40 votes for "state the reason flagged"; the SDK license episode.

P4

The Household Steward

The person who pays. Manages the family plan, fields the lockout texts, and decides in January 2027 whether double the old price was worth it.

Needs: proof. A factual annual account of what Premium actually did. The value summary is their surface.

Evidence: the Enhanced Premium repricing; the loyalty cohort stepping to full price; family-sharing demand.

Full persona treatment, including how each flagship phase adapts per persona: strategy/04-user-personas.md in the repository. Enterprise employee and admin personas are deliberately scoped to the Access Intelligence surface.
