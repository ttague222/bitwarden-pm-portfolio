# About this archive

These are the v1 documents, written **before** the primary research, preserved unedited.

They contain factual errors the research later corrected — most notably: Premium pricing (v1 assumed ~$10/yr; Enhanced Premium launched at $19.80/yr in January 2026) and the flagship premise (v1 proposed building a "Security Coach"; Bitwarden shipped vault health coaching in December 2025).

They are kept deliberately. The delta between this folder and the final assessment is documented in [../../reflection/23-how-the-research-changed-my-thinking.md](../../reflection/23-how-the-research-changed-my-thinking.md), and that delta — assumptions stated, tested against primary sources, and rebuilt where the evidence demanded — is the working method the repository exists to demonstrate.

`interim-top-five-opportunities.md` is the post-research interim analysis that the final discovery briefs (opportunities/) expanded and superseded.
