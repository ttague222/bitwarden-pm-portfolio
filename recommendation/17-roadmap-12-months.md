# Twelve-Month Roadmap Recommendation
## From the seat of the newly hired Senior PM (flagship owner), with the portfolio in view

*Assumes: one new Senior PM owning the flagship; existing teams own briefs 1, 3, 4, 5 with the specific contributions this assessment hands them. Quarters from hire date; all sizes are external estimates to be re-planned inside.*

---

## Q1: Repair, and learn the machine

**Flagship (own):**
- Week 1–2: the validation pulls (recommendation/15), especially the acknowledged warning-fix scope, renewal baselines, and the telemetry-tier conversation with security.
- Ship **Phase 1** (dismissal + per-item reasons) flagged with holdout, Experiment 3, the program's load-bearing test.
- Draft the methodology document; internal review with the vault-health engineers and security team.

**Portfolio contributions (hand off, don't own):**
- Brief 5: deliver the passkey failure taxonomy (Experiment 1, analysis of existing issues, no build) to the passkeys PM.
- Brief 1: propose the capture-funnel instrumentation spec (Experiment 6) to the extension team.

**Exit:** H1 read (does agency increase resolution?); warning-sentiment trend; taxonomy delivered.

## Q2: Transparency in public

**Flagship:**
- **Phase 2:** methodology RFC published with pre-registered success criteria; comment window; disposition table; GA of the "How we decide" links from every warning.
- Begin **Phase 3** instrumentation: renewal survey (Experiment 4) live; annual-value-summary build starts (Experiment 11).

**Portfolio:**
- Brief 3: contribute the narration pattern, publish the tags rollout plan on the 487-vote thread with the item-model team.
- Brief 5: error-message rewrite (Experiment 7), cheap, high-signal.

**Exit:** RFC health metrics; ≥1 community proposal adopted; renewal survey collecting.

## Q3: The renewal dashboard quarter

**Flagship:**
- **Phase 3** complete: value-attribution readout by bundle item; the January renewal dashboard live for leadership; annual value summary shipping to subscribers ahead of the renewal window.
- Phase 4 discovery only: moderated sessions on guided-resolution concepts; pilot spec written against Q1–Q2 evidence.

**Portfolio:**
- Brief 1: fill-assist expansion readout (Experiment 8, run by extension team) informs the heuristic-vs-corpus strategy.
- Brief 3: duplicate *report* pilot (Experiment 9) if the item-model team's capacity allows, the assessment's highest-value risk-avoidance test.

**Exit:** leadership has receipts (or honest confidence intervals) for January.

## Q4: Renewal, readout, and the earned extension

**Flagship:**
- **January 2027 renewal cohort readout**, the program's accountability moment, reported with the loyalty-step-up split and stated measurement tiers.
- **Phase 4 pilot** (Experiment 15: guided resolution paths) ships flags-first *only if* Q1–Q3 evidence justified it; measured on resolution, holdout maintained.
- Quarterly re-rank of the opportunity portfolio with a year of internal evidence; publish the methodology changelog v2.

**Portfolio:**
- Brief 4 enters its window: with encryption-v2 further along, scope the unlock reliability track with the platform team (second-year territory, as assessed).
- Brief 5: compatibility-matrix pilot decision (Experiment 12) based on Q1 taxonomy + Q2 error-message results.

**Exit:** renewal verdict; extension go/no-go made on evidence; year-two charter proposal.

---

## What this roadmap deliberately does NOT do

- Does not put the new PM on brief 1 (cross-team quality program, needs executive sponsorship, not a new owner), brief 4 (crypto-sequenced), or ahead of the in-flight tags work (brief 3).
- Does not schedule AI-agent productization, watch item pending demand evidence; revisit at the Q4 re-rank.
- Does not promise dates to the community for anything not already committed, the research is unambiguous about what broken public expectations cost here.

## Dependencies and standing risks

Coordination with the in-flight internal warning fix (week-one discovery); security-team review gates on the methodology doc and any telemetry; DevRel bandwidth for the RFC; encryption-v2 timeline volatility (isolated by design, the flagship never touches the crypto path); the honest possibility that renewal attribution stays confounded (fallback: leading indicators, stated plainly).
